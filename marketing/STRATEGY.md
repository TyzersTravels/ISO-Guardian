## 1. Market Analysis

### The Opportunity
- **70,000+ companies** in SA hold or are pursuing ISO certification (SABS data)
- ISO 9001 is **mandatory for government tenders** (PPPFA compliance)
- Mining Charter requires ISO 45001; manufacturing exporters need ISO 14001
- Most SA SMEs still manage compliance on **paper, spreadsheets, or email** — no dedicated software
- Competitors charge R5,000-R15,000/mo and target enterprise. We undercut at R2,000/mo for SMEs

### Target Customer Profiles (ICPs)

| ICP | Title | Company Size | Pain Point | Where They Are |
|-----|-------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| **Primary** | Quality Manager / SHEQ Manager | 50-500 employees | Drowning in paper, audit panic, no single system | LinkedIn, WhatsApp groups |
| **Secondary** | Operations Director / MD | 20-200 employees | Need ISO cert for tenders, don't know where to start | LinkedIn, Facebook, Google |
| **Tertiary** | Procurement Manager | Enterprise | Needs suppliers to prove ISO compliance | LinkedIn |
| **Partner** | ISO Consultants / SHEQ firms | 1-20 employees | Need a platform to manage multiple clients | LinkedIn, WhatsApp, referrals |

### Key Industries (SA)
1. **Manufacturing** — ISO 9001 for exports, ISO 14001 for environmental
2. **Construction** — ISO 45001 mandatory for large contracts
3. **Mining & Resources** — Mining Charter compliance, safety-critical
4. **Food & Beverage** — ISO 22000/9001 for retail supply chains
5. **Engineering & Professional Services** — Tender requirements
6. **Government Suppliers** — PPPFA requires quality management proof

### Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Mango (SA) | ~R8,000/mo | Enterprise-only, complex onboarding |
| Qualio | $500+/mo (USD) | Not SA-focused, no ZAR pricing, no POPIA |
| MasterControl | Enterprise | Overkill for SMEs, no local support |
| Spreadsheets/Paper | "Free" | No audit trail, manual, error-prone |

**ISOGuardian's edge:** SA-built, ZAR pricing, POPIA-compliant, 10-minute setup, R2,000/mo.

## 11. Video Marketing Strategy

### Recommended Tools (Budget-Conscious)

| Tool | Cost | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| **Google Veo (Gemini)** | Free with Google One AI Premium / pay-per-use | AI-generated video from text prompts, product showcases, abstract visuals |
| **OBS Studio** | Free | Screen recordings of ISOGuardian platform |
| **CapCut** | Free | Editing Reels/TikToks/Shorts, add captions + transitions |
| **Canva Video** | Free tier / R150/mo Pro | Branded video templates, social media sizes |
| **InVideo AI** | Free tier / ~R300/mo | Generate marketing videos from text prompts |
| **Synthesia** | ~$30/mo | AI avatar spokesperson for explainer videos |

### Google Veo / Gemini for Video

Google's Veo (accessible through Gemini Advanced) can generate short video clips from text descriptions. Best uses for ISOGuardian:

1. **Abstract brand clips** — "Generate a video of digital documents connecting to each other with glowing lines, professional dark purple and cyan theme" → use as intro/background for LinkedIn video posts
2. **Product concept visuals** — "Animated network of ISO compliance documents flowing between interconnected nodes" → visualise the cross-reference system
3. **Social media backgrounds** — Short looping clips for Instagram Stories and Reels backgrounds

**Limitations:** Veo generates ~8-second clips. Best used as B-roll/backgrounds combined with screen recordings and text overlays, not as standalone marketing videos.

### Video Content Calendar

**Week 1:** Screen recording — Template marketplace walkthrough (60s, LinkedIn + YouTube)
**Week 2:** Gemini Veo clip — Document neural network animation + voiceover about cross-references (30s, Instagram Reel)
**Week 3:** "POV: Audit in 2 weeks" — talking head + screen recording showing download flow (45s, LinkedIn)
**Week 4:** Client testimonial or case study (when available) (90s, all platforms)

### Production Workflow (Zero Budget)

1. **Script** — Write 60-second script based on LinkedIn post copy above
2. **Screen record** — OBS Studio recording of isoguardian.co.za template marketplace
3. **B-roll** — Generate 2-3 clips in Google Veo/Gemini (abstract compliance visuals)
4. **Edit** — CapCut: combine screen recording + Veo clips + text overlays + music
5. **Export** — 9:16 for Reels/Stories/TikTok, 16:9 for LinkedIn/YouTube, 1:1 for Facebook
6. **Post** — Cross-post with platform-specific captions

### Video Dos and Don'ts

- DO show the actual product (screen recordings build trust)
- DO add captions (80%+ of social video is watched on mute)
- DO keep it under 60 seconds for social (90s max for LinkedIn)
- DO use the purple/cyan brand colours in overlays
- DON'T use AI avatars pretending to be real employees (breaks trust)
- DON'T claim "AI-powered" or "guaranteed certification" in video content
- DON'T use copyrighted music (CapCut has free commercial-use tracks)
