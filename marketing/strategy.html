Brand Voice

  • Professional but approachable — not corporate jargon, not too casual
  • Confident — we know ISO compliance, we've built the solution

  • Empathetic — we understand audit stress and compliance fatigue

3. Channel Strategy

LinkedIn Primary — Highest ROI for B2B

Goal: Position Tyreece as ISO compliance thought leader; drive demo requests  |  Frequency: 4x/week (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri)

Content mix: 40% educational, 25% pain point, 20% product, 15% personal/founder story

  • Personal profile posts (Tyreece) outperform company page 5–10x
  • Comment on ISO/quality management posts daily (15 min/day)
  • Join and engage in groups: "ISO 9001 Practitioners", "SHEQ South Africa", "SA Manufacturing"
  • Connect with 20 quality managers per week (personalized note)
  • Weekly poll or question to drive engagement

Facebook Secondary — Awareness + Community

Goal: Build community, drive website traffic  |  Frequency: 3x/week

Content mix: Share LinkedIn posts (adapted), client wins, tips, memes/relatable content

  • Create "ISO Compliance South Africa" Facebook Group (community play)
  • Post in existing groups: "SHEQ Professionals SA", "SA Small Business Network"
  • Use Facebook Events for free webinars
  • Cross-post from LinkedIn with slight adaptation

Instagram Brand Building — Visual Authority

Goal: Brand awareness, attract younger quality professionals  |  Frequency: 3x/week (feed) + daily Stories

Content mix: Branded tip cards, before/after, behind-the-scenes, Reels

  • Carousel posts (swipe-through tips) get highest engagement
  • Reels: 30–60s "ISO Tip of the Day" or "Audit Horror Stories"
  • Stories: Polls, Q&A, day-in-the-life, feature previews
  • Hashtags: #ISOCompliance #ISO9001 #QualityManagement #SHEQ #SouthAfricanBusiness #SME

X / Twitter Thought Leadership — Industry Commentary

Goal: Industry visibility, engage with SABS/ISO news  |  Frequency: 5x/week (short-form, low effort)

Content mix: Hot takes on compliance news, quick tips, thread breakdowns

  • Quote-tweet SABS, ISO, and industry news with commentary
  • Weekly thread: "ISO [Standard] Clause [X] explained in plain English"
  • Engage with #ISO9001 #QualityManagement hashtags
  • Tag relevant accounts (SABS, quality professionals)

WhatsApp Direct — Highest Conversion in SA

Goal: Direct lead nurturing, client communication  |  Frequency: 2–3 broadcast messages per week, daily Status updates
  • WhatsApp Business profile with catalog (showing platform features)
  • Broadcast lists segmented: Prospects, Active Leads, Partners, Clients
  • Status: Daily tips, feature highlights, testimonials (disappears in 24h — FOMO)
  • Quick reply templates for common questions
  • WhatsApp group for "ISO Compliance SA" community (max 1024 members)

Google SEO + Google Business Profile

Goal: Capture "ISO compliance software South Africa" searches
  • Google Business Profile (free, shows in Maps/Search)
  • Target keywords: "ISO 9001 software South Africa", "compliance management tool", "SHEQ software", "ISO audit management"
  • Blog content on landing page (future: /blog route)
  • Landing page already has JSON-LD, meta tags, sitemap
