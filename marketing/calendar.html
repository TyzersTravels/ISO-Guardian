Monthly Review Checklist

At the end of each month, review the following:

  • Which posts got the most engagement? Create more like those.
  • How many leads came through the ISO Assessment?
  • How many consultation requests?
  • How many trial signups?
  • What questions are people asking? Turn those into content.
  • Which platform drives the most website traffic?
Track everything. The monthly review is where you refine your strategy. Double down on what works, cut what doesn't. By month 3, you'll have a clear picture of which platforms and content types drive real leads.
