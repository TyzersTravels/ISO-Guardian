What Works For You

  • No real SA competitor at this price point — the market is underserved
  • Government tender demand — businesses NEED ISO cert, it's not optional
  • Reseller model — partners scale distribution faster than solo selling
  • Low churn category — compliance software is sticky (3% monthly churn)

What Works Against You

  • Zero brand recognition — trust takes 3-6 months to build
  • No testimonials yet — social proof is critical in compliance
  • SA market is small — ~15K businesses actively seeking software
  • Budget sensitivity — even R2,000/mo goes through the MD at most SMEs
  • Solo founder — limited hours in the day for sales + support + development
