## 2. TERM AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Fixed Term**
This Agreement shall commence on the Commencement Date and continue for the Initial Term of twelve (12) months, unless terminated earlier in accordance with Clause 10.

**2.2 Renewal**
Upon expiry of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew on a month-to-month basis at the then-current Monthly Subscription Fee, unless either party provides at least thirty (30) days' written notice of non-renewal prior to the end of the current term or renewal period.

**2.3 CPA Cooling-Off**
Where the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008 applies (including distance selling or direct marketing transactions), the Client may cancel this Agreement within five (5) business days of signing for a full refund of any amounts paid, less any prorated usage fees for the period of actual use.

## 3. SUBSCRIPTION FEES AND PAYMENT

**3.1 Monthly Payment**
The Client shall pay the Monthly Subscription Fee as specified in Schedule A, due in advance on the first (1st) of each calendar month. All amounts are in South African Rand (ZAR) and are exclusive of Value-Added Tax (VAT) at the prevailing rate.

**3.2 Payment Method**
Payment shall be made via electronic funds transfer (EFT) to the bank account specified by ISOGuardian. ISOGuardian reserves the right to introduce automated payment processing (debit order or card payments) with thirty (30) days' prior notice.

**3.3 Late Payment**
If payment is not received within seven (7) days of the due date, ISOGuardian may restrict access to the Platform. If payment is not received within fourteen (14) days, ISOGuardian may suspend the Client's account. A late payment fee of 2% per month (compounded) on outstanding amounts may be charged.

**3.4 Price Adjustments**
ISOGuardian may adjust the Monthly Subscription Fee once per annum with a minimum of thirty (30) calendar days' written notice. Annual increases shall not exceed 10% or the prevailing Consumer Price Index (CPI) plus 2%, whichever is greater, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

**3.5 No Refunds for Partial Periods**
No refunds shall be given for partial months or unused portions of the subscription, except where required by the CPA or where cancellation occurs during the cooling-off period described in Clause 2.3.

## 4. EARLY TERMINATION

**4.1 Early Cancellation by Client**
The Client may initiate early cancellation either via the in-app Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel Subscription flow, or by providing sixty (60) days' written notice to support@isoguardian.co.za. ISOGuardian may terminate this Agreement with immediate effect if the Client commits a material breach (including non-payment exceeding thirty (30) days, violation of acceptable use policies, or breach of POPIA obligations) and fails to remedy such breach within fourteen (14) days of receiving written notice thereof.

**4.3 Termination for Cause by Client**
The Client may terminate this Agreement without penalty if ISOGuardian commits a material breach and fails to remedy such breach within thirty (30) days of receiving written notice, including failure to maintain the Service Levels specified in Clause 6.

## 5. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

ISOGuardian shall provide the Client with access to the Platform, which includes the following core features as at the Commencement Date:

1. **Document Management:** Upload, store, organise, and retrieve ISO-related documents with automated document numbering (IG-[CLIENT_CODE]-DOC-XXX format), company-scoped storage, and Activity Trail logging for ISO 7.5.3 compliance.
2. **Non-Conformance Report (NCR) Management:** Create, assign, track, and close NCRs through the full lifecycle (Open, In Progress, Closed) with root cause analysis, corrective action tracking, and branded PDF export per ISO 9001:10.2.
3. **Audit Management:** Schedule, execute, and document internal and external audits with findings, observations, evidence documentation, and close-out procedures per ISO 19011:2018.
4. **Management Review Module:** Schedule, conduct, and document management reviews with full meeting minutes, decisions, action items, resource decisions, and improvement opportunities per ISO 9001:9.3.
5. **Compliance Tracking and Scoring:** Real-time compliance scoring across ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018, organised by clause for audit readiness.
6. **Activity Trail:** Comprehensive audit logging of all system actions (uploads, edits, status changes, deletions, archives) with company-scoped data isolation for ISO 7.5.3 traceability.
7. **Branded PDF Exports:** Professional branded document exports for NCRs, Audit Reports, and Management Review Minutes, featuring the Client's company logo, document control numbering, and signature blocks.
8. **Data Export:** Export compliance data and records in standard formats for external use and auditor requirements.

**Important Disclaimer:** ISOGuardian is a management tool designed to assist the Client with ISO compliance management. ISOGuardian does not guarantee ISO certification, audit success, or 100% compliance with any standard. The Client remains solely responsible for achieving and maintaining compliance with all applicable ISO standards and regulations.

## 6. SERVICE LEVELS

**6.1 Platform Availability**
ISOGuardian shall use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain Platform availability of at least 99% per calendar month, measured as the percentage of total minutes in the month during which the Platform is accessible. This target excludes:

- Scheduled maintenance (communicated at least 48 hours in advance where practicable)
- Force majeure events as defined in Clause 12
- Client-side connectivity or browser issues
- Third-party service provider outages (including Supabase, Vercel, or Cloudflare)

**6.2 Service Credits**

If Platform availability falls below 99% in any calendar month (excluding the exclusions above), the Client shall be entitled to a service credit as follows:

| Monthly Uptime | Service Credit (% of Monthly Fee) |
|---|---|
| 99.0% – 100% | No credit |
| 95.0% – 98.9% | 10% credit |
| 90.0% – 94.9% | 25% credit |
| Below 90% | 50% credit |

Service credits are applied to the next billing cycle and do not accumulate. The maximum credit in any month shall not exceed 50% of the Monthly Subscription Fee. Service credits are the Client's sole and exclusive remedy for failure to meet availability targets.

**6.3 Support**
ISOGuardian shall provide email-based support during business hours (Monday to Friday, 08:00 to 17:00 SAST, excluding South African public holidays). Response timeframes:

| Priority | Description | Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | Platform inaccessible or data loss risk | 4 business hours |
| High | Core feature unavailable | 8 business hours |
| Medium | Feature degraded, workaround available | 2 business days |
| Low | General enquiry or cosmetic issue | 5 business days |

## 7. DATA PROTECTION AND POPIA COMPLIANCE

**7.1 Roles**
For the purposes of POPIA, the Client is the Responsible Party and ISOGuardian is the Operator with respect to any personal information processed through the Platform on behalf of the Client.

**7.2 Data Hosting and Security**
All Client data is hosted on Supabase infrastructure in the European Union (London region), compliant under Section 72 of POPIA. Security measures include:

- Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Row Level Security (RLS) for multi-tenant data isolation
- Role-based access controls
- Comprehensive audit logging of all data access and modifications
- Cloudflare Turnstile CAPTCHA protection
- Session management with automatic timeout

**7.3 Data Ownership**
The Client retains all rights, title, and interest in data uploaded to or generated within the Platform. ISOGuardian is granted a limited licence to store, process, and display such data solely for providing the Service.

**7.4 Data Breach Notification**
ISOGuardian shall notify the Client without undue delay (and within 72 hours) upon becoming aware of any unauthorised access to the Client's personal information, in compliance with Section 22 of POPIA.

**7.5 Data Retention and Deletion**
Upon termination, ISOGuardian shall retain Client data for thirty (30) days to allow export. After this period, all data shall be permanently deleted unless retention is required by law.

**7.5.1 Right to Erasure**
During the subscription term, the Client may request erasure of personal information via the in-app Account Settings → Request Data Deletion flow, or by emailing support@isoguardian.co.za. ISOGuardian shall comply within 30 days, subject to lawful retention obligations under POPIA s14 (record-keeping), tax legislation, or regulatory requirements. Data required for legal retention will be anonymised or segregated from active use.

**7.6 Data Subject Requests**
ISOGuardian shall provide reasonable assistance to the Client in responding to data subject requests under POPIA within prescribed timeframes.
