## 3. COMPENSATION & RESELLER STATUS

3.1 **Commission Structure:** The Reseller earns 25% of MRR for the lifetime of each Client's subscription, provided the Reseller remains in Good Standing. Commission does not apply to one-time setup fees or custom development charges, which are retained 100% by the Licensor.

3.2 **Reseller Internal Usage (Complimentary Licence):** While the Reseller remains in Good Standing under this Agreement, the Licensor grants the Reseller a complimentary, non-transferable, non-exclusive licence to use a Partner Admin instance of the Software for its own internal administrative and operational purposes. This licence includes: admin access, multi-client reseller dashboard, analytics, and up to ten (10) internal Reseller users. The Reseller may not use this instance to host, manage, or store the data of external Clients; all external Clients must subscribe under the paid tiers in Schedule B and execute a Client Subscription & Service Level Agreement. The complimentary licence automatically terminates on termination of this Agreement, or on a material breach by the Reseller (including IP infringement, breach of POPIA obligations under Clause 4, or breach of the IP provisions in Clause 2).

4.1 **Roles:** For the purposes of POPIA, the Reseller's Clients are the Responsible Parties for their own data. ISOGuardian is the Operator processing Client data on behalf of those Clients. The Reseller acts as an intermediary and, where it processes Client personal information in the course of onboarding or support, is itself bound by POPIA as a Responsible Party for that processing.

4.2 **Hosting:** Client data is hosted on servers located in the European Union (Supabase, London region), satisfying the transborder flow requirements of POPIA Section 72 via Standard Contractual Clauses.

4.3 **Breach Notification:** The Reseller must notify the Licensor within 24 hours of becoming aware of any potential data breach or unauthorised access to Client data.

4.4 **Client Agreements:** The Reseller shall ensure every Client executes a Client Subscription & Service Level Agreement prior to being granted access to the platform. The Reseller shall not grant or promise platform access before a signed agreement and first payment are received.
