## 4. PHASE 2: AGREEMENT & SIGNATURE

Once the client is interested, here is your step-by-step process:

1. **Step 1 — Select Tier:** Help the client choose the right tier based on their user count: Starter (1–10 users, R2,000/mo), Growth (11–20 users, R3,700/mo), or Enterprise (21+ users, custom pricing).
2. **Step 2 — Complete the SLA:** Fill in the client's details on the Client Subscription & SLA. Both parties sign. This locks them into a 12-month fixed-term agreement.
3. **Step 3 — Collect First Payment:** The client pays the first month's subscription via EFT. Payment must be received before platform access is granted.
4. **Step 4 — Submit to ISOGuardian:** Email the signed SLA and proof of payment to support@isoguardian.co.za with the subject line: **NEW CLIENT: [Company Name]**.
5. **Step 5 — Confirmation:** ISOGuardian will confirm receipt within 1 business day and begin platform setup.

**Important Legal Notes:**

- The client has a 5 business day cooling-off period under the CPA (distance selling)
- Never promise ISO certification — ISOGuardian is a management tool, not a guarantee
- All marketing materials must be pre-approved by ISOGuardian
- Do not offer unauthorised discounts — pricing is per Schedule B of the Reseller Agreement

## 6. PHASE 4: CLIENT HANDOVER & TRAINING (WEEK 1)

This is YOUR moment as the reseller. You are the face of the service.

**Day 1–2: Welcome Call (30–45 min)**

- Share login credentials securely (not via plain email — use WhatsApp or phone)
- Walk the client through their first login
- Explain the Dashboard, compliance scores, and navigation
- Show them how to upload a document and explain document numbering (IG-[CODE]-DOC-001)

## 8. PHASE 6: ONGOING RELATIONSHIP

After the first month, your role shifts to periodic check-ins and audit preparation support:

- **Monthly:** Quick check-in (5 min call or WhatsApp message) — any issues? Upcoming audits?
- **Quarterly:** Review compliance scores, suggest areas for improvement, discuss additional users
- **Pre-Audit:** Help client run through their Activity Trail, export key documents, ensure NCRs are closed
- **Renewal:** At months 10–11, confirm the client is happy and discuss renewal or upgrades
- **Referrals:** Happy clients are your best source of new business — ask for referrals

## 10. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What if a client wants to cancel early?**
A: The SLA has a 12-month fixed term. Early cancellation incurs a fee: 50% of remaining fees (months 1–6) or 25% (months 7–12). The CPA cooling-off period of 5 business days applies for new sign-ups.

**Q: Does ISOGuardian guarantee ISO certification?**
A: No. ISOGuardian is a management tool. Certification responsibility remains with the client. Never promise certification to prospects.

**Q: What if the client needs additional users?**
A: They can upgrade their tier at any time. Contact ISOGuardian to adjust the subscription.

**Q: How do I track my commission?**
A: Your Reseller Dashboard and Analytics page show real-time MRR, commission amounts, and client status.

**Q: What if a client reports a bug or issue?**
A: First verify it is a software bug and not user error (per your Tier 1 support obligations under the Reseller Agreement). Then log it with ISOGuardian at support@isoguardian.co.za with browser type, steps to reproduce, and screenshots. Critical issues are responded to within 4 business hours.

**Q: Can I offer custom pricing?**
A: No. All pricing follows Schedule B of the Reseller Agreement. Unauthorised discounts may void your commission on the affected client.

**Q: What about POPIA — whose responsibility is it?**
A: The client is the Responsible Party for their own data. ISOGuardian is the Operator. The platform handles the technical safeguards. You as Reseller are responsible for how you handle client personal information during the sales and onboarding process.
